About FUTMinna

The Federal University of Technology, Minna (FUTMinna) is a federal government university established in 1983 to provide specialized education in technology and applied sciences. Our institution is committed to excellence in teaching, research, and community service.

With over 20,000 students across various undergraduate and postgraduate programs, FUTMinna continues to produce graduates who are making significant contributions to national and global development.
